# ANTI GRAVITY

# PRODUCT DETAIL PAGE ARCHITECTURE

Version 1.0

---

# PURPOSE

The Product Detail Page is the heart of the Anti Gravity storefront.

It is where storytelling becomes purchasing.

The page should inspire confidence before asking for commitment.

Every section should answer a customer's next question before they need to ask it.

The page should combine cinematic presentation, technical detail, premium interaction design, and frictionless purchasing into one continuous experience.

---

# DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

The Product Detail Page should feel like:

A luxury product presentation

↓

A hands-on product experience

↓

A technical specification sheet

↓

A guided purchasing journey

↓

A confidence-building checkout gateway

The experience should never feel like a traditional ecommerce template.

---

# PRIMARY OBJECTIVES

Present the product beautifully.

Communicate value.

Demonstrate craftsmanship.

Reduce uncertainty.

Increase trust.

Simplify purchasing.

Encourage exploration.

Maintain exceptional performance.

Every major section should answer a customer question:

---

# VISUAL LANGUAGE

Large imagery

Generous whitespace

Minimal distractions

Elegant typography

Subtle motion

Consistent spacing

Clear hierarchy

---

# RESPONSIVE EXPERIENCE

Desktop

Immersive multi-column presentation.

Tablet

Balanced hybrid layout.

Mobile

Thumb-friendly single-column experience with persistent purchase actions.

Content should adapt rather than simply shrink.

---

# ACCESSIBILITY

Support:

Keyboard navigation

Screen readers

Reduced motion

High contrast

Zoom

Touch accessibility

Semantic structure

Readable typography

---

# PERFORMANCE

Load essential purchase information immediately.

Defer secondary content until needed.

Lazy load galleries, videos, reviews, and advanced 3D experiences.

Maintain smooth scrolling and interaction throughout the page.

# ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA

The Product Detail Page is complete when:

Customers can confidently evaluate a product without leaving the page.

Purchasing actions remain accessible at all times.

The page balances storytelling with technical information.

Every section is reusable and CMS-driven.

The experience remains fast, accessible, responsive, and visually consistent with the Anti Gravity design language.

The Product Detail Page should become one of the defining experiences of the Anti Gravity brand, combining luxury presentation with exceptional usability.
